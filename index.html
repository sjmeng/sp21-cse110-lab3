Day 2: Going downhill

Sherbet has gone on a bit of a bender recently. I think he has some arsonist tendencies because every time I leave for groceries, the apartment is on fire when I get back. Honestly super annoying because I didn't register a lot of my valuables with my homeowner's insurance yet. Also, Drax fell into one of the interdimensional portals that keep appearing on the walls and floors every time Sherbet gets frustrated that he's not allowed to chase the birds outside our window. They sound like this.

He's fine now, but I'm seriously considering a spray bottle for Sherbet if this behavior continues. And it's not like I'm going to give in and let him chase the local fauna. He may be an interdimensional being, but I will not let him be an eco-terrorist.
